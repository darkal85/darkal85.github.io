Hi, I’m Dariusz Kalociński


I am an assistant professor in the Institute of Philosophy at the University of Warsaw, affiliated with the Department of Logic. Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Social Models of Semantics Learning: Acquisition and Evolution of Quantifier Meaning project, led by Nina Gierasimczuk, and funded by the Polish National Science Centre (NCN).

My research interests lie in language learning and language evolution, especially in the context of more abstract semantic entitites such as quantifiers. I am also interested in logic, computability theory and algorithmic randomness.
